How to learn Japanese?


Of course, the best way to learn everything would be to hire a tutor, but that's not everything. There a lot of websites in the internet where you can learn any language you want, and Japanese is one of them. I recommend you don't start off by learning Japanese writing system because it is quite difficult. It is advisable to focus more on communication at early stages.

Here are ways to learn Japanese:


1.TV Shows



TV Shows are so helpful when trying to learn a language. Without even realizing it you will
be able to memorize words in no time. Of course use subtitles when watching them.


Movies & Series


Anime

If you are into them, Anime are so entertaining
and will help you develop your language.

Some Japanese Words

Animals

'Dōbutsu' means Animals

'Neko' means Cat

'Inu' means Dog

'Uma' means Horse

'Nezumi' means Rat

'Usagi' means Rabbit

'Tori' means Bird

'Raion' means Lion

'Tora' means Tiger

'Ushi' means Cow

'Zō' means Elephant

Conversation skills

'Konnichiwa' means Hello

'Ohayō' means Good Morning

'Konbanwa' means Good Evening

'Oyasumi Nasai' means Goodnight

'Arigatō' means Thanks

'Sumimasen' means Sorry

'Onegaishimasu' means Please

'Hai' means Yes

'Ie' means No

'Mata Ne' means See You Again

'Wakarimashita' means I Understand

Food names

'Kuimon' means Food

'Asa Gohan' means Breakfast

'Chūshoku' means Lunch

'Mizu' means Water

'Pan' means Bread

'Tori Niku' means Chicken Meat

'Gohan' means Rice

'Itadakimasu' means Let's Eat

'Gochisousama Deshita' means Thanks For The Food

'Onaka Suita' means I'm Hungry
