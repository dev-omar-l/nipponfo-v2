Places to visit in Japan?


Best places to visit in Japan


Japan is one wonderful country for sure. But let's say you find a way to travel there, and you want to make sure you use your trip to the fullest extent.

Here I will be showing you the places you must not miss when you travel to 'Nippon':

Akihabara, Tokyo



Akihabara is a heaven for gamers and Otakus. Basically, anything
game and anime related will be found in Akihabara.


Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Kyoto



Kiyomizu-dera is a wonder in Kyoto, which is the former capital of Japan.
That means it consists of more traditional architectures than Tokyo.


Himeji Castle, Himeji City



Himeji castle in Himeji City is so marvelous. It is one of the only few castles
that weren't rebuilt due to age, which means it is still in its original state!


Naoshima Island




Located in Japan's Seto inland Sea. It is known for its many museums.
It's most symbolic structure is the yellow pumpkin.


Okayama City



Okayama is home for some of the best natural views
not only in Japan but in the whole world.

Asakusa



Asakusa is located in Tokyo, but still holds on to its traditional
culture. Definitely a must-visit especially in the winter.



That concludes all the places you must definitely visit. Again keep in minds those are just few examples.
There is still much more, that's why Japan is very unique compared to the rest of the world.
